Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Information Technology (BSc. CSIT) is a four year course affiliated to Tribhuvan University designed to provide the student with all sorts of knowledge in the field of Information Technology and Computing. The program develops the underlying principles of both Computer Science and Information Technology and shows how these principles can be applied to real world problems.
